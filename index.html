Dear valued sponsor,

Thank you for choosing to support us on our journey to provide the best version of Porama that we can. We appreciate your commitment to our gaming project.

In order to ensure a smooth and efficient experience for all parties involved, we kindly request that you adhere to the following terms of service (ToS) when making a financial contribution to Porser:

Cancellation Policy

Should you decide to cancel your sponsorship, please inform the Porser team at least one week in advance. This will allow us to make the necessary arrangements and ensure a seamless transition.

Ownership of Funds

Porser only accepts financial contributions from funds that are legally owned by the sponsor. We do not accept funds that have been obtained through illegal or unethical means, such as theft or fraud.

Third-Party ToS

When making a financial contribution via PayPal, you must accept and abide by their terms of service. The same applies when making a contribution via Ionos.

Accepted Forms of Payment

Porser only accepts financial contributions via PayPal or Ionos. We do not accept any other forms of payment at this time.

We hope these ToS are clear and straightforward. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Best regards,

Porser team
